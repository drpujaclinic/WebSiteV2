Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026 · DPDP Act 2023 Compliant

Dr. Puja's Clinic ("we", "our", or "Clinic") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices in strict compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 of India.

1. Data We Collect

When you use our online booking form or reach out via digital channels, we collect: Full Name, Contact Phone Number, Email Address, and Health Reason / Purpose of Consultation (provided voluntarily).

2. Purpose of Collection & Processing

Your personal information is processed solely for explicit, specific, and legitimate medical purposes: facilitating appointment bookings, sending essential clinic communications (confirmations, reminders, rescheduling alerts), and answering direct inquiries submitted by you.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Processors

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data is securely handled only by our chosen technical subprocessors: Formspree (contact form submissions) and WhatsApp (Meta) only when you voluntarily initiate communication via our click-to-chat links.

4. Data Retention Period

We retain your digital data only as long as necessary to fulfill the medical scheduling purpose, or to comply with standard medical record retention regulations under Indian healthcare laws.

5. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act, 2023

As a Data Principal, the DPDP Act grants you the following rights, exercisable at any point:

  • Right to Access: Summary of personal data being processed by us.
  • Right to Correction & Erasure: Correct inaccuracies or request erasure of personal data no longer necessary for scheduling.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for digital communications at any point.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Register a complaint regarding data processing with the clinic before escalating to statutory boards.
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate another individual to exercise rights on your behalf in the event of death or incapacity.

6. Data Requests & Contact

To exercise your data privacy rights, withdraw consent, or file a request, please contact our Data Protection Officer at: drpujasclinic@gmail.com
