Our Services

Obstetrics & Pregnancy

High-Risk Pregnancy Management

Expert care for complicated pregnancies including hypertension, diabetes, multiple gestations, placenta praevia, and IUGR — with close monitoring throughout.

Normal Delivery (NVD)

Supportive, low-intervention approach to normal vaginal delivery with continuous monitoring and expert judgement throughout labour.

C-Section (LSCS)

Safe, planned or emergency caesarean delivery by an experienced surgeon with careful post-operative recovery management.

Antenatal Care

Structured pregnancy monitoring from confirmation to delivery — including regular scans, blood work, weight monitoring, and nutrition counselling.

Book Consultation →

Complicated Pregnancy

Management of placenta praevia, premature labour, gestational diabetes, oligohydramnios, and other complications requiring specialist oversight.

Book Consultation →

Postpartum Care

Postpartum check-ups, lactation support, contraception counselling, and management of postpartum complications including haemorrhage and infection.

Book Consultation →

Infertility Treatment

Infertility Evaluation

Systematic investigation of infertility causes including hormonal panels, HSG (tubogram), semen analysis, and pelvic sonography to identify and treat the root cause.

Book Consultation →

Ovulation Induction

Medical stimulation of ovulation using clomiphene, letrozole, or gonadotrophins — carefully monitored to maximise success and minimise risk.

Book Consultation →

IUI (Intrauterine Insemination)

Assisted conception procedure to increase fertilisation chances — a well-established first-line treatment for select infertility presentations.

Book Consultation →

Recurrent Pregnancy Loss

Thorough investigation and management of repeated miscarriages — including thrombophilia screen, uterine evaluation, and immunological testing.

Book Consultation →

PCOD / PCOS Management

PCOD Diagnosis & Management

Comprehensive evaluation and long-term management of polycystic ovarian disease including dietary guidance, hormone therapy, and cycle regulation.

Book Consultation →

Hormonal Disorder Treatment

Diagnosis and treatment of hormonal imbalances causing irregular periods, weight changes, acne, hair loss, and other symptoms.

Book Consultation →

Menstrual Irregularities

Investigation and treatment of irregular, heavy, or painful periods — addressing the root cause rather than just managing symptoms.

Book Consultation →

Gynaecological Surgery

Laparoscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive keyhole surgery for fibroids, endometriosis, ovarian cysts, and ectopic pregnancy. FMAS-certified with faster recovery.

Hysteroscopy

Gynaecological Endoscopy

Safe MTP

Safe, confidential, non-judgmental medical termination of pregnancy as permitted under the MTP Act — handled with complete patient privacy.
